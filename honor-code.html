Honor Code

The following is adapted from the BYU Honor Code.

All participants of this conference will seek to demonstrate those moral virtues encompassed in the gospel of Jesus Christ, and will:

  • Be honest
  • Live a chaste and virtuous life
  • Obey the conference rules
  • Use clean language
  • Respect others
  • Abstain from alcoholic beverages, tobacco, tea, coffee, and substance abuse
  • Participate conference activites and church meetings
  • Observe Dress and Grooming Standards
  • Wear wristbands for all events

(notes on billeting rules)

Most importantly, be safe and have fun!
